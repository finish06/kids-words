## What Worked

- **Cycle-11 scope + timebox discipline.** One-item cycle, pre-ranked hypotheses (H1-H4), 30-min investigation cap, 3-run stability gate. Root cause isolated at ~25 min; shipped, merged, book-kept inside 2h.
- **v0.2.0 tag + CHANGELOG cut cleanly.** Went from "staging serves 0.2.0, repo tagged v0.1.0, pyproject stale at 0.1.0" to everything aligned + tagged + deployed + verified, same session. User-prompted mid-cycle ("Check version at staging URL"); didn't derail the main cycle.
- **Staging verification after each push.** `/api/version` and `/api/health` checked immediately after each redeploy. Caught nothing (staging was healthy throughout), but the habit itself is valuable.

## What Didn't

- **Local/remote main divergence after squash-merge.** Avoidable: the roadmap commit `5ad4a6a` should have been pushed before branching. Cost: ~3 min to diagnose + recover.
- **`learnings.md` still isn't JSON** — Action #3 from the 2026-04-12 retro is deferred a second time. Smart filtering by stack/category remains impossible.
- **PRD ↔ milestone-file sync drift** was only caught because a roadmap command forced a read of both. Needs a periodic check, not ad-hoc discovery.
